# Divine Spectrum: Design System & Visual Identity

## 1. Color Palette
The **Divine Spectrum** palette fuses ancient regality with futuristic minimalism.

### A. Primary Colors
- **Void Black**: `#050505` (Main background, deep space)
- **Abyss Dark**: `#0c0a09` (Panel backgrounds, cards)
- **Stone Grey**: `#1c1917` (Headers, interactive backgrounds)

### B. Secondary Colors
- **Carbon**: `#333333` (Subtle borders, inactive states)
- **Stardust**: `#9ca3af` (Secondary text, metadata)
- **Mist**: `#6b7280` (Disabled states, placeholders)

### C. Accent Colors (Functional)
- **Divine Gold**: `#D4AF37` (Primary accent, active states, focus)
- **Mythos Orange**: `#E3963E` (Brand highlight, logo, calls to action)
- **System Red**: `#ef4444` (Danger, Combat, Strength)
- **Tech Cyan**: `#06b6d4` (Data, Dexterity, Speed)
- **Aether Purple**: `#a855f7` (Magic, Manipulation, Power)

### D. Highlights
- **Solar Flare**: `#fbbf24` (High luminosity gold)
- **Crimson Glow**: `#dc2626` (Combat active states)

### E. Backgrounds
- **Deep Layer**: `#000000` with `bg-opacity-40` overlay.
- **Card Layer**: `#0c0a09` with thin border.
- **Modal Layer**: `backdrop-blur-md` with `bg-black/80`.

## 2. Typography
A duality of the archaic and the machine.

### A. Font Families
- **`font-ritual`**: Serif/Display (e.g., Cinzel). Used for: Headers, Names, Titles, "PANTHEONEXUS".
- **`font-tech`**: Monospace/Futuristic (e.g., Share Tech Mono). Used for: Stats, Tiers, Data, "LGN 5", "EXP".
- **`font-body`**: Sans-serif (e.g., DM Sans/Inter). Used for: Descriptions, Lore text.

### B. Hierarchy
- **H1 (Page Title)**: `font-ritual`, Uppercase, `tracking-[0.2em]`, Text-Gold.
- **H2 (Section)**: `font-ritual`, Uppercase, `tracking-widest`, White.
- **H3 (Card Title)**: `font-ritual`, Bold, White.
- **Label**: `font-tech`, Uppercase, `text-xs`, `tracking-wider`, Gold/Gray.
- **Body**: `font-body`, `text-sm`, `leading-relaxed`, Gray-300.

## 3. Patterns & Textures
### A. Backgrounds
- **Concrete/Grain**: A subtle noise texture overlay (`opacity-40`, `mix-blend-multiply`) adds tactile realism.
- **Sacred Geometry**: Thin, hairline geometric overlays (circles, rhombuses) in `white/5` or `gold/5`.
- **Hex/Cubes**: Used in headers and specialized panels (`opacity-10`).

### B. Overlays
- **Vignette**: Radial gradient from transparent to black at edges.
- **Scanlines**: Horizontal lines (`bg-size: 100% 4px`) used on "tech" elements like the map or terminal.

## 4. Gradients
- **Golden Hour**: `linear-gradient(to right, #D4AF37, #E3963E)` (Used for progress bars, active borders).
- **Void Fade**: `linear-gradient(to bottom, transparent, #050505)` (Used for image fade-outs).
- **Attribute Spectrums**: Unique gradients for attributes (e.g., Red to Transparent for Strength).

## 5. Borders & Lines
- **Standard**: `1px solid #333333`.
- **Active/Focus**: `1px solid #D4AF37` (Gold).
- **Ornamental**: Double lines or borders with "caps" at corners.
- **Separators**: Very low opacity gradients (`from-transparent via-gray-800 to-transparent`).

## 6. Glow & Shadow Effects
- **Divine Glow**: `shadow-[0_0_20px_rgba(212,175,55,0.3)]` (Gold).
- **Tech Glow**: `shadow-[0_0_15px_rgba(6,182,212,0.2)]` (Cyan).
- **Danger Glow**: `shadow-[0_0_15px_rgba(239,68,68,0.4)]` (Red).
- **Deep Shadow**: `shadow-2xl` or `drop-shadow-lg` for floating panels.

## 7. Icons & Symbols
- **Library**: `lucide-react`.
- **Style**: Geometric, Stroke-width 1.5 or 2.
- **Behavior**:
    - **Inactive**: Gray/Mist (`text-gray-500`).
    - **Active**: Gold (`text-[#D4AF37]`) or Attribute Color.
    - **Hover**: Scale up (`scale-110`), increase brightness.

## 8. Layout & Spacing
- **Grid System**: 12-column fluid grid.
    - **Mobile**: 1 column.
    - **Tablet**: 2 columns (`md:`).
    - **Desktop**: 3-4 columns (`lg:`, `xl:`).
- **Spacing Tokens**: Based on 4px scale (`p-1`, `p-2`, `p-4`, `gap-4`).
- **Container**: `max-w-7xl` centered.

## 9. Component Styling
### A. Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid Gold (`bg-[#D4AF37]`), Black Text, Uppercase, Font-Tech/Ritual, Bold.
- **Secondary**: Outline Gold (`border-[#D4AF37]`, `text-[#D4AF37]`), Transparent bg.
- **Ghost**: Transparent, Gray Text, Hover White.
- **Action**: Small, Square/Icon-only, `h-8 w-8`.

### B. Cards
- **Base**: `bg-[#0a0a0a]`, `border border-[#333]`.
- **Interactive**: Hover `border-[#D4AF37]/50`, Hover `bg-[#111]`.

### C. Inputs
- **Style**: Dark background (`bg-black`), Border (`border-[#333]`), Focus (`ring-1 ring-[#D4AF37]`).
- **Text**: `font-mono` (Tech feel).

## 10. Visual Inspiration
- **Name**: **Divine Spectrum** (also referred to as *Panopticon Interface*).
- **Aesthetic**: **Retrofuturista + Arcaico**. A fusion of Ancient Greek Mythos (Columns, Gold, Rituals) and Cyberpunk/Sci-Fi (Holograms, Data streams, Terminals).
- **References**:
    - *Assassin's Creed (Odyssey/Valhalla)*: Menu navigation, cursor styles, item rarity colors.
    - *Quironax / Deus Ex*: Black and Gold palette, geometric ornamentation.

## 11. Animations
- **Transitions**: `duration-300`, `ease-in-out`.
- **Hover**: Subtle lift (`-translate-y-1`), Glow increase.
- **Expansion**: `framer-motion` layout animations (smooth height adjustment).
- **Pulse**: Used for "Live" status or "Active" alerts.

## 12. Special Effects
- **Glitch**: Text hover effects (optional).
- **Scanlines**: Overlay on maps and tactical feeds.
- **Blur**: `backdrop-blur-md` on floating panels (Glassmorphism).
- **Blend Modes**: `mix-blend-overlay` for textures.

## 13. Accessibility
- **Contrast**: High contrast (Gold on Black, White on Black) meets WCAG AA.
- **Focus**: Distinct gold rings on focusable elements.
- **Sizing**: Touch targets min 44px on mobile.
- **Feedback**: Hover states, Active states, and Toast notifications for all actions.

## 14. Theme
- **Mode**: **Dark Mode Only** (The Void).
- **Atmosphere**: Mysterious, Powerful, Ancient, Technological.

## 15. Overall Identity
The application feels like a discovered artifact—an ancient tablet powered by an alien or divine operating system. It treats the user not just as an admin, but as an **Oracle** or **Operator** of reality. Every interaction should feel weighty, deliberate, and ritualistic.
